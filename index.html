Overuse of water

The overuse of water can lead to the depletion of life underwater through various interconnected mechanisms. Excessive water extraction for agricultural irrigation, industrial processes, and domestic use can result in reduced river and lake levels, disrupting the natural flow of water and diminishing essential habitats for aquatic organisms.
